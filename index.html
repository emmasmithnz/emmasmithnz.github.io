About Emma

Emma is passionate about putting horses first and can help you understand your horse better as well as keep them feeling their best. Emma prioritizes a well-rounded, holistic approach in her work. With 10 years of experience working with horses, Emma specialises in young and problem horses. She offers a personalised approach tailored to meet both you and your horse's needs.

- She is a Certified Equine Body Worker (EEBW) - completing 300+ hours of study and practicals both in person classes/lectures/hands on and online study and externship, including a certificate in equine anatomy - 100+ hours.
